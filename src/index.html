About Grove Blind & Shutters Co.

The Grove Blind & Shutter Co. is a proudly family-owned and operated business, built on the values of trust, craftsmanship, and exceptional service. With deep local roots and decades of industry experience, we’re passionate about helping homeowners transform their spaces with beautifully crafted, made-to-measure shutters and blinds.

We operate from two key locations to ensure outstanding local service. Our established Tyldesley showroom serves customers in Astley, Tyldesley, Worsley, Bolton, and the surrounding areas, while our newly opened head office and showroom in Lowton extends our reach to Leigh, Culcheth, Golborne, Warrington, and beyond. No matter where you visit us, you’ll be met with expert advice and a warm, personal approach.

With over 25 years of hands-on experience in custom blind and shutter installation, our dedicated team understands how to balance style, function, and budget to deliver the perfect window solution for every home. We’re not just fitters—we’re craftsmen who take pride in every detail, from your first consultation to the final fitting.

Every project we undertake is personally overseen by the owners, ensuring consistent quality, accountability, and care. We believe in doing things right the first time and treating each customer like part of our extended family.

For your peace of mind, all of our products come with a comprehensive manufacturer’s warranty. In the rare event that something does go wrong, you can rest assured that we’ll resolve the issue quickly and professionally—no hassle, no excuses.
