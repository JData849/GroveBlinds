About Us

About Grove Blind & Shutters Co.

At Grove, we believe that beautiful homes begin with beautiful shutters. As specialists in premium, made-to-measure shutters - and providers of bespoke blind solutions - we bring over 25 years of experience into every home we visit.

Founded as a family-run business, Grove was built on the values of trust, craftsmanship, and exceptional service. We don't operate showrooms - instead, we offer expert in-home consultations, bringing our design knowledge, material samples, and tailored advice directly to you.

While shutters are our signature, our expertise extends across a curated range of stylish, functional blinds - ideal for modern living and available in an array of custom finishes to suit your space and budget.

With every project personally overseen by the owners, you can expect:

  • A personalised experience, never sales pressure
  • High-end, handcrafted products that stand the test of time
  • A reputation for meticulous installation and ongoing aftercare

    • And for your peace of mind, all of our products are backed by a comprehensive manufacturer’s warranty and our own hands-on support -should anything ever need attention.

    Whether you're looking to elevate your home with plantation shutters or need blinds that blend style and practicality, Grove delivers with care, quality, and pride.
