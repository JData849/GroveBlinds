About Us

Our Story

At Grove Blind & Shutter Co., we’ve built our reputation on more than just exceptional products-we’ve built it on people, pride, and purpose.

As a family-run business, our roots run deep in Greater Manchester. What began as a small operation has grown into two full-service showrooms, serving homes across Tyldesley, Lowton, and beyond.

From day one, our mission has been simple: to offer honest advice, beautifully crafted products, and an experience that leaves every customer feeling at home.

Why Choose Us

Beautiful Blinds, Perfect Fit, Personal Touch

We're more than just installers - we're your trusted local specialists in made-to-measure blinds and shutters. Here's why homeowners choose Grove Blind & Shutter Co. to transform their spaces:

  • Family Values

    • We’re a family-owned business, not part of a franchise or faceless chain. When you choose us, you’re dealing directly with the people who built this company from the ground up. From your first consultation to the final installation, you’ll experience genuine care, honesty, and accountability.

  • 25+ Years of Experience

    • With over 25 years in the industry, we’ve honed our craft to deliver exceptional quality and service. Our team has the expertise to handle any project, big or small, ensuring your blinds and shutters are fitted perfectly every time.












  • Truly Local

    • We’re proud to serve Greater Manchester and surrounding areas. Our local knowledge means we understand the unique needs of our community, and we’re always just a phone call away for support or advice.


  • Made to Measure

    • Every home is unique, and so are our solutions. We offer a wide range of made-to-measure blinds and shutters tailored to your specific requirements. Our team will work with you to find the perfect style, material, and fit for your space.

  • Peace of Mind

    • We believe in transparency and integrity. That’s why we offer clear, upfront pricing with no hidden costs. Our team will guide you through every step of the process, ensuring you feel confident and informed from start to finish.
